## Story

**As a** professor,  
**I want** the system to distinguish internal grades from published grades,  
**so that** students only see results after an explicit broadcast action.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-1: Internal versus published state is modelled
**Given:** grades are not immediately visible to students  
**When:** grade publication foundation is implemented  
**Then:** editable `grade_entries` and derived `calculation_results` remain internal, while portal-visible data is stored only in immutable `publication_snapshots`

### AC-2: Broadcast is the publication trigger
**Given:** portal visibility is linked to explicit human action  
**When:** the professor starts a publication  
**Then:** a `broadcast_job` is created and only a confirmed publication action can create current `publication_snapshots`

### AC-3: Snapshot is linked to delivery tracking
**Given:** WhatsApp remains the principal broadcast channel  
**When:** publication is executed  
**Then:** each snapshot is linked to the originating `broadcast_job`, and delivery outcomes are recorded per recipient in `notification_deliveries`

### AC-4: Re-publication after change is supported
**Given:** grades and dates may change after initial publication  
**When:** the professor republishes  
**Then:** a new immutable snapshot version is created, only one snapshot per student/context is current, and previous snapshots remain auditable

### AC-5: Student reads only current published data
**Given:** students do not see internal history  
**When:** the portal read model queries grade data  
**Then:** it is limited to current published snapshots and never reads `grade_entries` or draft calculation results directly

### AC-6: Formula remains configurable
**Given:** the official academic formula and derived statuses are not closed  
**When:** calculations are stored for publication  
**Then:** the model records `formula_version`, configurable component results and provisional derived states without hard-coding institutional final status rules

## Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Students see draft/internal notes | Portal endpoints must query only `publication_snapshots` |
| Broadcast partially fails | Record per-recipient state in `notification_deliveries`; do not erase snapshots without explicit rollback policy |
| Formula is implemented as final too early | Store configurable/versioned calculation output and mark derived states provisional |
| Re-publication loses audit trail | Immutable snapshots; new current version instead of destructive overwrite |

## Dev Notes

### Core rule
No grade becomes visible in the portal before the related explicit broadcast/publication action creates a current snapshot.

### Open formula decision
The story must not close the official academic formula. Use configurable assessment definitions and store a `formula_version`; product validation will later decide exact score calculation and derived statuses.
